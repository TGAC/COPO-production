Getting started

This page relates to the Barcoding manifest component () of a profile. Barcoding manifests (also known as targeted sequences) are assembled and annotated sequences representing interesting features or gene regions.

Download blank manifest: Select the desired checklist from the dropdown menu above then, click the button next to it to download a blank manifest.

Upload completed manifest: After having completed the manifest, choose the matching checklist option from the dropdown menu then, click the button to upload it.
